Transportation
Airports
We recommend flying into either SFO (San Francisco International Airport) or OAK (Oakland International Airport), depending on where you are staying. Both are on public transportation lines.
Getting Around the Area
Renting a car is not necessary. Between public transportation (BART between SF and Oakland, and MUNI within SF) and Lyft (Uber’s friendlier rival), there are decent transit options for getting where you will need to go for the weekend.
